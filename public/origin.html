ORIGIN

This is a formula style car designed keeping in mind SUPRA 2015. It was the first racing car manufactured by our club, which came with its own ups and downs. We stood at 67th position among the 167 teams that participated in the competition at the Madras Motor Track

ENGINE
600 CC
88 kW
66 nm
TOP SPEED
115 km/h
MAX POWER
88 kW
MAX TORQUE
66NM

The car was designed to reach speed up to 115 kmph powered by a Honda CBR 600cc engine with max torque of 66Nm. This car reached 0 to 100 kmph in 14.5 sec. Firstly the car had a green body but after the competition, the team did many changes to its design and the body was also changed to Red-Black
